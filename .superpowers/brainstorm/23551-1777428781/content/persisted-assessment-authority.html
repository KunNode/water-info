Display Contract

Normal

  • Use latest persisted AI assessment.
  • All pages display the same risk level and assessed time.

Degraded

  • If no record, show “no AI assessment yet”.
  • If stale or fetch fails, show stale/offline state and optional alarm-only fallback label.
